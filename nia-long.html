Nia Long is an American actress, voice actress, producer, and director who is known for her television roles like playing Officer Sasha Monroe in the NBC crime drama series Third Watch, Katherine “Kat” Speakes in the soap opera Guiding Light, Katie Graham in the ABC comedy-drama series Big Shots, E.A.D. Shay Mosley in the CBS action drama series NCIS: Los Angeles, Lisa Wilkes in the NBC sitcom The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air, Andrea Solomon in the CBS legal drama series Judging Amy, Neika Hobbs in the Netflix comedy-drama series Dear White People, Vanessa Walker in the legal comedy-drama series Boston Legal, Roberta Tubbs (voice) in the animated sitcom The Cleveland Show, Billie Page in the legal drama series The Divide, and Lavette in the Netflix sitcom blackAF. Moreover, her film credits include 47 Meters Down: Uncaged, Roxanne Roxanne, Look Both Ways, Fatal Affair, The Best Man Holiday, Good Hair, The Single Moms Club, Big Momma’s House, Big Momma’s House 2, Are We Done Yet?, and Life in a Year.
