Trayce Thompson Parents and British Family Background

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1083 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Trayce Thompson parents are Mychal Thompson and Julie Thompson. Trayce is part of the England team in the World Baseball Classic 2023.

His dad, an accomplished NBA player, laid the groundwork for his young ones to embrace the beautiful game and blaze their trail of excellence.

Mychal developed them as elite players making waves in American sports. Trayce is a professional baseball pitcher who pledges his loyalty to Los Angeles Dodgers in MLB. 

Meanwhile, his two siblings, Mychel and Klay, are distinguished basketball players who have achieved remarkable success in their careers.

The 31-year-old Dodgers star played for Great Britain in the 2023 World Baseball Classic despite being born and bred in the United States.

His father, Mychal, was originally from the Bahamas, a British constitutional monarchy nation.

Trayce Thompson Family Background

Trayce Thompson’s family is deeply entrenched in a legacy of athletic greatness. His mom and dad both share track records of impeccable athletic past.

Mychal Thompson, his father, is undoubtedly a star player of his generation playing basketball for several different NBA franchises, such as the Los Angeles Lakers, San Antonio Spurs, and Portland Trail Blazers. 

Meanwhile, his mother, Julie, played collegial volleyball for the University of San Francisco.

Mychal Thompson

Mychal is a retired American basketball player from the Bahamian capital of Nassau. 

Buoyed by his passion and dedication, he began playing basketball at a young age. To attain more feats and play in competitive leagues, Mychal migrated to America in his teens. 

Mr. Thompson made his home in the vibrant city of Miami, Florida, and joined a nearby school Miami Jackson Senior High. With an impressive school career, he joined the University of Minnesota to further enhance his skills and resilience.

Playing for four seasons with the Minnesota Golden Gophers, Mychal listed his name in the 1978 NBA Draft. Impressed by his college stats, the Portland Trail Blazers picked him in the first draft.

In 1986, he moved to the Spurs, where he spent a season before joining Los Angeles Lakers the following year. 

When reflecting upon the hallmark accomplishments that defined his career, Mychal proudly recalls acquiring two NBA championship titles, both winning during his illustrious tenure with the Lakers in 1987 and 1988.

Following his retirement, Mychal has remained active in sports. He now works as a radio host and commentator for the Lakers’ radio.

Julie Thompson

Trayce Thompson mother Julie played varsity volleyball during her college days. After a noteworthy volleyball stint at Ridgefield High, she embarked on a professional path, playing for the University of Portland and San Francisco.

Julie met Mychal at the gymnasium at the University of Portland, and they tied the wedding knot in 1987.

With a deep-seated appreciation for the value of athletics, Julie nurtured an environment encouraging their children to pursue their passions in the sporting arena.

How Is Trayce Thompson British?

Trayce represented Great Britain in the World Baseball Classic in March 2023. Trayce has British ancestry from his father Mychal who was born and raised in the Bahamas.

As a result, Trayce was eligible to play for the United Kingdom in the WBC, despite not having any direct ties to the country.

Trayce Thompson Siblings

Trayce has two siblings named Mychel Thompson and Klay Thompson. He grew up with his brothers in Los Angeles, California. 

It is understandable where the athletic prowess of Tryace and his two brothers stemmed from because of their guardians’ flawless pedigree of sporting achievement.

As a result, all three sons were able to carve out their unique path to greatness, achieving their own level of stardom in their chosen sports.

The battle for the pro spotlight among the three continues as each one is trying their best to carve out their name in the annals of American sports history. 

Mychel Thompson

Mychel is a former basketball player who played shooting guard for Pepperdine University.

Born on the 1st of June in 1988, the 34-year-old stands at a towering 6 feet 6 inches in height. 

Following a promising start to his high school basketball career at Jesuit High School in Portland, Oregon, Mychel opted to transfer to Santa Margarita Catholic High School in Margarita, California.

Then in 2007, Mychel attended Pepperdine University, where he joined the campus basketball team Pepperdine Waves. Playing three seasons in NCAA leagues with the Waves, he further developed his skills and reached new heights of achievement on the court.

However, Mychel went undrafted in the 2011 NBA draft. Then the NBA G league side Erie BayHawks signed him in November 2011.

He played in the development league for seven years till 2017, winning the NBA D-League title in 2015 with the Santa Cruz Warriors.

Klay Thompson

Trayce Thompson brother Klay is a four-time NBA champion basketball player who plays for the Golden State Warriors. He is one of the highest-paid players in the league.

Just like Trayce, Klay Thompson is also not British but can represent the United Kingdom in basketball because of his father’s roots.

Since getting selected in the 2011 NBA Draft, Klay has been the heart of the Warriors’ remarkable run to win The Larry O’Brien Trophy four times in the last seven seasons.

Born 8 February 1990, Klay has achieved numerous accolades, including five NBA All-Star selections, two All-NBA Third Team, and a gold medal with the United States men’s basketball team at the 2016 Olympic Games.

His love for the game was apparent from an early age, and he nurtured his skills at Santa Margarita Catholic High School, where he followed in his older brother’s footsteps.

After a successful high school career, Klay went on to attend Washington State University, where he played for the Cougars and emerged as one of the top college basketball players in the country.

Trayce Thompson Wife

Trayce Thompson wife Jillian Thompson is a former soccer player at Northern Michigan University. 

Jillian has kept her Instagram private and is absent from most of the social media platforms because she prefers a low-key personal life without a buzz from media outlets.

However, she made a rare public appearance before the World Baseball Classic tournament in March 2023.

In a special messages video posted by the official LA Dodgers page on Twitter, she was seen with their child conveying a good luck note to Trayce. 

In the late spring of 2021, Thompson and his significant other, Jillian, welcomed a new addition to their family. The awe-inspiring and charming Baby Thompson was born in July, signifying a momentous occasion for the couple.
