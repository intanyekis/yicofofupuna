How long should you keep two strand twists in?

Two strand twists can last a long time! Although this style can be time-consuming to create, they typically stay intact for 2-6 weeks. Washing, conditioning and deep conditioning the style will keep twists looking fresh longer and keep hair healthy.

To help you get that picture perfect style past day one, here’s what we suggest: Do your initial twist out on wet hair. Although doing a twist out on dry hair will give you more length, if you twist while your hair is wet, it will set better.

Do twists damage your hair?
