'Peaky Blinders': The Cast Ranked From Richest To Poorest

 Posted on July 8, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1304 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Peaky Blinders, a British historical crime drama television series that Steven Knight created, has captivated viewers all over the world with its compelling storytelling, intricate characters, and period-appropriate set design. Set in the aftermath of World War I in Birmingham, England, the show follows the notorious Shelby crime family as they rise to power in the world of organized crime.

The global success of Peaky Blinders can be attributed in large part to its stellar ensemble cast, with each member bringing depth and authenticity to their respective roles. Cillian Murphy's portrayal of the cunning and enigmatic leader, Thomas Shelby, has become iconic, drawing audiences into his complex world of power struggles and personal demons. Alongside Murphy, the performances of Helen McCrory as the formidable matriarch, Polly Gray, and Paul Anderson as the volatile Arthur Shelby, have been widely praised for their intensity and nuance.

Moreover, the show's attention to detail in recreating the gritty atmosphere of 1920s Birmingham has been lauded by critics and viewers alike, immersing audiences in a world of smoky pubs, cobblestone streets, and period-appropriate costumes. The meticulous set design and cinematography further enhance the immersive experience, transporting viewers back in time to an era defined by post-war tensions and the rise of organized crime. Below, we will take a look at how these characters are doing in real life, particularly their net worth.

10 Peaky Blinders Cast Member Tom Hardy (Alfie Solomons) - $55 Million Net Worth

The charismatic Tom Hardy portrays the enigmatic Alfie Solomons. Alfie is a Jewish gang leader with a penchant for witty dialogue and calculated moves. His complex alliance with the Shelby family adds a layer of intrigue to the series, as his unpredictable nature blurs the line between friend and foe. Hardy's portrayal of Alfie is a standout, capturing his eccentricities, humor, and underlying sense of danger.

9 Peaky Blinders Cast Member Helen McCrory (Aunt Polly Gray) - $25 Million Net Worth

Helen McCrory brought an undeniable presence to the role of Elizabeth "Polly" Gray, the matriarch of the Shelby family. Polly is a formidable and shrewd character, overseeing the family's legitimate business operations while maintaining a watchful eye over her nephews' criminal endeavors. Her strength, intelligence, and unwavering loyalty make Polly a beloved and respected character. McCrory's performance provided a perfect balance of vulnerability and resilience, creating a character who was as relatable as she was powerful.

Sadly, Helen McCrory passed away in 2021 at the age of 52. She did not make it into the final season, however, Cillian Murphy was sure to pay tribute to her prior to it.

Cillian Murphy has amassed a $20 million net worth over the years. His character is the heart of Peaky Blinders, Thomas Shelby. Thomas, also known as Tommy, is the cunning and ambitious leader of the Shelby family. A war veteran with a strategic mind, he possesses a charisma that draws both allies and enemies alike.

Lizzie's journey from a marginalized position to a position of influence within the family showcases her resilience and determination. O'Keeffe's performance adds depth to Lizzie's character, highlighting her transformation and her complex relationship with Tommy.

4 Peaky Blinders Cast Member Sophie Rundle (Ada Shelby) - $3 Million Net Worth

In the show Peaky Blinders Sophie Rundle plays Ada Shelby, the only daughter of the Shelby family. 2 Peaky Blinders Cast Member Finn Cole (Michael Gray) - $2 Million Net Worth
