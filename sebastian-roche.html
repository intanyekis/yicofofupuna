Sebastian Roché is a Scottish-French actor, voiceover artist, stage actor, and writer who is known for having played some amazing characters in several shows and movies such as Mikael in both The Vampire Diaries and The Originals, Pierre Desjardins in The Namesake, Jerry Jacks in General Hospital, Balthazar in Supernatural, and Martin Heusmann in The Man in the High Castle.

Education

Sebastian Roché attended Cours Florent, a private French drama school located in Paris, as well as the École Nationale Supérieure des Arts et Techniques du Théâtre. Later, he got enrolled at the French National Academy of Dramatic Arts from where he graduated in 1989.
