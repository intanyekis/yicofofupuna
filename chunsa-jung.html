Chunsa Jung Facts

  • In 2016, she made her first theatrical film appearance by playing the role of Hopia in the romantic comedy-drama film Just the 3 of Us.
  • Chunsa Jung brought to life the character of Laurie in the 2016 romantic comedy-drama film The Third Party, starring Angel Locsin, Sam Milby, and Zanjoe Marudo.
  • She joined the 2nd season of the television singing and impersonation competition show Your Face Sounds Familiar Kids in 2018 and went on to impersonate various celebrities including Janet Jackson, Psy, Meghan Trainor, Pink, Rihanna, Katy Perry, Madonna, Taylor Swift, and Shakira.
  • From 2019 to 2020, Chunsa Jung was cast as Celestia in the fantasy drama television series Starla.
