Donnie Wahlberg of 'Blue Bloods' Shows Excitement for 'Yellowstone' Season 3 and Fans React

 Posted on July 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  381 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Actor Donnie Wahlberg is happy about the return of a TV show that he loves seeing. Although he doesn't star on the TV show, he couldn't keep the excitement to himself.

Donnie Wahlberg recently shared a tweet about his favorite show. According to his tweet, he will be busy during the Summer and Fall watching "Yellowstone" and "Bluebloods." He wrote,

"Wooooohooooo! My favorite show (that I do not act in) is coming back!. @Yellowstone through the entire summer, then hand the baton off to @BlueBloods_CBS when it comes back in the fall! Can't wait!."

Donnie Wahlberg attends Jenny McCarthy's SiriusXM show from Grant Park in Chicago, IL before the NFL Draft, in Chicago, Illinois. | Photo: Getty Images

Since his tweet, the actor's followers have commented on his choice of movies, and many of them share the same opinion with the star.

"I have watched Blue Bloods from day one and Yellowstone. I love them because there's a story to them."

Another fan wrote, "I freakin love this show. Huge fan of Kevin Costner. My absolute favorite old school movie with him in it tho has to be Robin Hood Prince of Thieves." A third person wrote,

"Adding to our watch list for Fall 2020."

"Yellowstone" is all about a family, The Duttons, who lives in Montana, and will protect their ranch at all costs. John Linson and Taylor Sheridan created the show.

"Blue Bloods," on the other hand, is on its tenth season, and actor Tom Selleck, who played Frank Reagan in the show, said that it doesn't look like it is ending anytime soon.

Donnie Wahlberg is the eighth of nine children, and his younger brother is also an actor, Mark Wahlberg. He had his acting debut in 1996 in a movie that also starred actor Mickey Rourke and late rapper Tupac Shakur.

He later had more prominent roles in movies like "Detective Eric Matthews" and in the second, third, fourth, and fifth entries in the popular horror series "Saw."

Donnie Wahlberg at the premiere of HBO's "Band of Brothers" at the Hollywood Bowl. | Photo: Getty Images

His most prominent roles have been in TV shows, like "The Police Dramas," "Boomtown," "Blue Bloods," and the short-lived thriller "Runaway." He is married to Jenny McCarthy, and they share two sons together.
