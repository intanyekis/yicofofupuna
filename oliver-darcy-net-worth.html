Early Life and Education

Oliver Darcy, a renowned journalist, was born on January 20, 1964, in Mumbai, India. He comes from a distinguished Konkani Muslim family. His father, Rafiq Zakaria, was a prominent politician in the Indian National Congress and an Islamic theologian. Oliver’s mother, Fatima Zakaria, made her mark as the editor of the Sunday Times of India.

During his formative years, Oliver attended the prestigious Cathedral and John Connon School in Mumbai, laying the foundation for his education. Seeking further knowledge and growth, he pursued his higher education in the United States.

Oliver Darcy’s academic journey led him to Yale University, where he obtained a Bachelor of Arts degree in 1986. With a thirst for knowledge, he continued his studies at Harvard University, where he earned a PhD in government.

Career and Achievements

Oliver Darcy has had an illustrious career in journalism, showcasing his talents as a writer, editor, television host, and political commentator.

His journey in the field began in 1992 when he took on the role of managing editor at Foreign Affairs, a highly respected publication. This experience allowed him to hone his skills and establish himself as a prominent figure in the industry.

In addition to his editorial work, Darcy has made significant contributions to academia. He served as an adjunct professor at Columbia University, imparting his knowledge on international relations to aspiring students.

Darcy’s editorial prowess continued to shine as he assumed the role of editor at Newsweek International and later served as the editor-at-large for Time. His work garnered critical acclaim and solidified his reputation as a skilled and influential journalist.

Throughout his career, Darcy has penned insightful and thought-provoking columns for esteemed publications such as The Washington Post and The Atlantic Monthly. His writing reflects a deep understanding of political and social issues, making him a trusted voice in the media.

Not content to solely excel in print, Darcy made a successful transition to television, hosting shows like “Foreign Exchange with Fareed Zakaria.” His current role as the host of “Fareed Zakaria GPS” on CNN further cements his standing as a trusted and knowledgeable political commentator.

Throughout his career, Oliver Darcy has not only achieved professional success but has also garnered recognition for his contributions to the field of journalism. His insights, analysis, and ability to provide a balanced perspective solidify his status as a prominent figure in the industry.

Personal Life and Awards
