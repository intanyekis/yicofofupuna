8 Did Tristan Thompson Cheat On Jordan Craig With Khloé Kardashian?

Before Tristan Thompson was in his very public relationship with Khloé Karsashian, Thompson was seeing lifestyle blogger and social media influencer Jordan Craig. The two dated for two years, breaking up some time in 2016. They broke up around the same time Thompson started dating Kardashian.

The timeline of the split and Thompson’s new relationship caused fans to speculate if there was any crossover. Craig’s pregnancy with Thompson’s son Prince only made matters worse. “Jordan does question the timing of Khloé’s very public timeline of events,” a source revealed. Thompson and Kardashian have denied these claims, but the transition from one girl to the next certainly made fans question his character.

7 Thompson Cheated On Khloé While She Was Pregnant

While Tristan Thompson and Khloé Kardashian were expecting their first child together, True, Thompson was busy elsewhere. In April 2018, the same month Kardashian was expected to give birth, Thompson was caught getting cozy with a mystery woman in a club in Manhattan.

Footage was captured by other club members of the two looking very close. In the video, viewers can see the woman lean in to Thompson and supposedly kiss the NBA player. Fans of Kardashian were outraged that Thompson would cheat on her, especially considering she was only days away from giving birth to their child.

6 Fans Call Tristan Thompson A Serial Cheater

The instance in New York was not the only time Tristan Thompson crossed the line while Khloé Kardashian was pregnant with their child. In April 2018, while footage was being released of Thompson in Manhattan, TMZ released footage of Thompson in Washington D.C. at a hookah bar.

The footage was from October 2017 and depicted Thompson with two girls. In the video, he kisses and touches both women. Khloé was only three months pregnant at the time. “He’s a serial cheater. And there will be more women to come out of the woodwork,” a source told People.

5 Tristan Thompson Cheated With Jordyn Woods, Kylie Jenner’s Best Friend

Despite Tristan Thompson’s cheating scandals while she was pregnant, Khloé Kardashian chose to stay with Thompson for their daughter. Fans did not agree with the decision, but Kardashian finally hit her breaking point with Thompson after he was caught with Jordyn Woods.

Woods was very close with the Kardashian family due to her friendship with Khloé’s younger sister, Kylie Jenner. Woods admitted to kissing Thompson but denied that any further actions took place between them. The betrayal hit hard for Kardashian considering how close Woods was with the family, and fans reacted similarly to the news.

4 Did Tristan Thompson Bribe Jordan Craig?

Tristan Thompson’s drama with Jordan Craig was not over. In 2019, amid the Jordyn Woods scandal, Thompson and Craig entered a custody battle over their son Prince. Craig claimed during a deposition that Thompson had previously paid her $112,000 to not hook up with other men even though he was seeing Khloé Kardashian at the time.

“That was part of a bribe,” Craig told Thompson’s attorney, Peter Lauzon, during the deposition. Craig claimed Thompson “would help me if I wasn’t, like, dating anybody or talking to anybody and that I would have to come see him.” Thompson denied this, but fans are outraged over the basketball player’s controlling ways.

3 Tristan Thompson And Lamar Odom’s Instagram War

In 2021, Tristan Thompson made a shot at Lamar Odom in the comments of one of Khloé Kardashian’s Instagram photos. NBA star Lamar Odom married Kardashian in 2009, but she filed for divorce in 2013. They never had children together and divorced due to Odom cheating on her and his drug abuse.

When Odom called Kardashian a “hottie,” Thompson unleashed his anger. Thompson replied, “God brought you back the first time. Play if you want, different results.” The comment was in reference to Odom’s almost-fatal overdose back in 2015, and fans thought Thompson had gone too far with the threat.

2 Tristan Thompson Cheated On Khloé With Maralee Nichols

Serial cheater Tristan Thompson strikes again. After getting back together with Khloé Kardashian in 2021, Thompson almost immediately strayed from the relationship. As detailed in the Hulu show The Kardashians, Kardashian threw Thompson a 30th birthday party. Afterwards, met up with Maralee Nichols and cheated on Kardashian.

Fans were beyond outraged to hear that Thompson had again failed Kardashian, especially after she had just forgiven him for the Jordyn Woods scandal. To make matters worse, Kardashian and Thompson had made the decision to have a second child through suragacy. Thompson again broke up their family while a child was on the way.

1 Is Tristan Thompson The Father Of Maralee Nichols’s Baby?

As if cheating on Khloé wasn’t enough, Maralee Nichols gave birth to Tristan Thompson’s child at the end of 2021. The pregnancy spurred Thompson to make a public statement about the affair, and he did not give Kardashian any warning. She had to find out at the same time as the rest of the world.

Thompson requested a paternity test for Nichols’s child, which ultimately proved that he is in fact the father of 6-month old Theo. Thompson and Nichols are currently involved in a child support lawsuit, as Thompson currently refuses to pay child support or even meet his newest child.
