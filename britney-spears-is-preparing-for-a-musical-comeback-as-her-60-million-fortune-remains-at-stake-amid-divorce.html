Britney Spears Is Preparing For A Musical Comeback As Her $60 Million Fortune Remains At Stake Amid

Britney Spears may have recently found herself newly single, but according to a new report, the singer is focusing her energy on a caeer comeback, including new music inspired by her troubled relationship with her family

According to The U.S. Sun, a confidential associate from Las Vegas has verified that the superstar has been discreetly composing lyrics over the past three years while contending with the constraints of her conservatorship, which had controlled her life.

More recently, her ongoing divorce from Sam Asghari is also apparently inspiring her new music.

According to the source, Sony Music is eager to see her back in the recording studio and has already extended a multi-million-dollar advance offer. They have also proposed the possibility of creating another Greatest Hits album, which may feature new tracks.

“Britney has been writing down lyrics and thoughts about the struggles of the last three years,” the insider told the publication. “Initially it was freeing herself from the conservatorship and now it’s about Sam and her splitting.”

"Sony has been talking to her for two years about creating a new record in her new era of life,” they continued. Britney hasn’t released a new album since 2019’s Glory.

“It has offered her access to significant writers and producers and a song catalogue sure to create No1s whenever she wants,” the source continued. “She says it is going to happen – soon. So new music is definitely coming.”

“And the talk too is that she could have a nice upfront payment of £4million ($5 million USD) just to jump back in and record vocals.

“A greatest hits package is an option for Sony, but that could include new material,” they added.

Britney currently has a net worth of $60 million, but her fortune is at stake amid her ongoing divorce from Sam Asghari, who filed to dissolve their marriage on August 16, citing irreconcilable differences.

There have been reports that Sam wants to challenge their prenup, which apparently only entitled him to $1 million after every 2 years of marriage, to be capped at $15 million after a decade. However, the couple were only married for 14 months before he filed for divorce.

There have rumors that Britney cheated on Sam with an employee, though other reports claim Sam’s team is trying to defame Britney in hopes of negotiating a higher divorce settlement.

Even though Britney hasn’t had to give Sam a payout yet, she’s reportedly paying $10,000 per month for his luxury apartment. Their divorce remains ongoing.
